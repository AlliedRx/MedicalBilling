Air Force COOL Credentialing Assistance

The Air Force Credentialing Opportunities On-Line (COOL) program helps active duty Air Force and Space Force members earn nationally recognized civilian credentials that align with their military training and occupation 芒聙聰 at no cost to the service member.

AlliedRx Institute is an approved provider under Air Force COOL. Our accelerated healthcare certification programs are designed to align with military occupational skills and help transitioning airmen build civilian careers in healthcare.

Visit AF COOL Portal 芒聠聮

Funding Amount

Up to $4,500

Per credential attempt, covering exam fees, training, and certification costs.

Who Qualifies

Active duty Air Force and Space Force members. Must have a valid military credential gap identified in the COOL system.

Approved Provider

AlliedRx Institute is an AF COOL-approved training provider. Programs are mapped to AFSC/MOS codes for seamless approval.

Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Apply

Active duty Air Force or Space Force member
Credential aligns with current or future AFSC
Must register through myLearning / AFVEC portal
Funding request approved prior to enrollment
No separation or retirement orders within 6 months
Sufficient time remaining on enlistment

Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) Program

The Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) program provides active duty Soldiers with up to $4,000 per fiscal year to pursue civilian credentials and certifications that complement their Military Occupational Specialty (MOS). The program is designed to enhance Soldiers' marketability after service and strengthen their current military skills.

AlliedRx Institute is registered on the Army COOL portal as an approved training provider. Our programs are mapped to relevant MOS codes so Soldiers can quickly verify eligibility and apply through the GoArmyEd / ArmyIgnitED portal.

Visit Army COOL Portal 芒聠聮

Annual Funding Cap

$4,000

Per fiscal year for eligible active duty Soldiers. Covers tuition, exam fees, and certification costs.

Who Qualifies

Active duty Army Soldiers (Regular Army, Army Reserve on active orders). Must have sufficient service time and credentials aligned with current or future MOS.

How Payments Work

Funds are paid directly to AlliedRx as the approved training provider. Soldiers are not required to pay out of pocket when approved.

Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Apply

Active duty Army Soldier (E-1 through O-10)
Credential must align with Soldier's MOS
Must apply through ArmyIgnitED portal
Minimum 2 years remaining on service contract
Commander approval required before enrollment
Not currently on a flagging action or APFT failure
How to Apply

Steps to Get Funded

1

Log Into ArmyIgnitED

Access the ArmyIgnitED portal and search for AlliedRx Institute under approved credentialing providers.

2

Select Your Program

Choose a healthcare certification that aligns with your MOS or transition goals and request CA funding.

3

Get Commander Approval

Obtain your commanding officer's signature. AlliedRx can assist with the required documentation.

4

Enroll at AlliedRx

Submit your approval to our military admissions team. Tuition is billed directly to the Army.

The My Career Advancement Account (MyCAA) Scholarship is a workforce development program that provides up to $4,500 in financial assistance to eligible military spouses who want to pursue portable careers 芒聙聰 careers that can move with the family as the service member is reassigned.

AlliedRx healthcare certifications are the perfect fit for MyCAA recipients. Our programs are portable, fast to complete, and lead to in-demand careers that thrive in any location 芒聙聰 whether near a base or across the country.

Visit MyCAA Portal 芒聠聮

Scholarship Amount

Up to $4,500

Total scholarship. Up to $2,000 per academic year for portable career programs.

Who Qualifies

Spouses of active duty service members in pay grades E-1 through E-5, W-1 through W-2, and O-1 through O-2 across all branches.

Portable Careers

AlliedRx programs qualify as portable careers 芒聙聰 healthcare credentials are recognized nationwide and remain valuable wherever military life takes you.

Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Apply

Spouse of active duty service member (all branches)
Service member is in pay grade E-1 to E-5, W-1 to W-2, or O-1 to O-2
Program leads to a portable, in-demand career
Enrolled in an approved school and program
Must create a MyCAA account on MilitaryOneSource
Financial assistance approved before classes begin
