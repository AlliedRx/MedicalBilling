Preparation Guide

Exam Success Strategy

The CMAA certification exam evaluates your readiness to manage the complex administrative demands of a modern healthcare facility.

Front-Office Topics to Master:

  • Scheduling: Triage, appointment types, and patient flow management.
  • Intake & Records: EHR documentation, demographic collection, and chart management.
  • Finance & Billing: ICD-10/CPT basics, insurance verification, and co-pay collection.
  • Compliance: HIPAA regulations, medical law, and OSHA front-office safety.
  • Professionalism: Telephone etiquette and medical business communication.
Study Reminder:
  • Review medical terminology roots/suffixes
  • Practice insurance claim life cycle steps
  • Understand basic bookkeeping & petty cash
  • Memorize patient privacy rights (HIPAA)
