Medical Assistant Roles & Responsibilities

Explore the diverse duties of medical assistants and their role as patient navigators.

Typical MA Responsibilities

Clinical Duties

  • Take vital signs
  • Prepare patients for exams
  • Assist with procedures
  • Administer medications
  • Perform basic lab tests

Administrative Duties

  • Schedule appointments
  • Manage medical records
  • Handle billing & coding
  • Answer phones
  • Verify insurance

Patient Navigator

  • Guide patients through healthcare system
  • Explain procedures & treatments
  • Assist with insurance questions
  • Coordinate referrals
  • Provide emotional support

Lab techs, radiologists, etc.

Scope of Practice vs. Standards of Care

Understand the legal and professional boundaries for medical assistants.

Key Definitions

Scope of Practice

Definition: The procedures, actions, and processes that a healthcare practitioner is permitted to undertake according to their professional license or certification.

  • Defined by state laws
  • Varies by state
  • Based on education & training
  • Legal boundary for practice

Standards of Care

Definition: The degree of care and skill that the average qualified healthcare professional would provide in similar circumstances.

  • Defined by professional organizations
  • Consistent across states
  • Based on best practices
  • Quality benchmark for practice
