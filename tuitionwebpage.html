Common Questions

Tuition FAQ

Everything you need to know about payment plans and enrollment.

Your registration fee ($49 for most programs, $99 for Pharmacy Tech) is due at enrollment. Your $99 down payment is not due until you add your class to your student portal — after completing orientation. This gives you time to get comfortable before payments start.

The Pharmacy Technician program has a $99 registration fee (versus $49 for other programs) due to the criminal background check and drug testing processing required for the program. This fee is non-refundable and is separate from tuition.
