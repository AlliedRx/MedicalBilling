Medical Administrative Assistant I

Our online Medical Administrative Assistant program offers you a robust curriculum that provides you with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the fast-paced healthcare environment. You will also be educated on medical law and ethics, healthcare confidentiality, ethical considerations in a medical setting, and the procedures to ensure compliance with industry standards.

02
Medical Administrative Assistant II

This course builds on foundational administrative skills with advanced scheduling, insurance verification, billing intake, and patient account management. Students will learn electronic health record (EHR) navigation, claims processing, daily operations, and medical emergencies. Upon completion, students will be prepared for the NHA CMAA certification exam.
