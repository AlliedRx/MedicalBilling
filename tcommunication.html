Therapeutic Communication Scenarios

Evaluate realistic patient interaction scenarios and select the most therapeutic response. Learn why certain communication approaches build stronger patient relationships and improve care outcomes.

💭 Scenario 1: Patient Anxiety About Procedure
A patient is visibly anxious about an upcoming blood draw. They mention they've had bad experiences in the past and are worried. What is your most therapeutic response?
👂 Scenario 2: Patient Shares Personal Concerns
During vital signs, a patient shares that they're struggling with stress at home and having difficulty sleeping. They seem to want to talk. How do you respond?
😢 Scenario 3: Patient Receiving Difficult News
The physician tells a patient they need additional testing because of abnormal results. The patient becomes tearful and upset. You're helping with follow-up. What's your most therapeutic response?
