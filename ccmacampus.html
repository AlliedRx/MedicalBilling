As a CCMA, you will perform both clinical and administrative duties â from taking vital signs and drawing blood to managing patient records and scheduling appointments.

Curriculum

2 Courses Â· Complete Curriculum

Each course builds on the last â from foundational knowledge to advanced clinical procedures.

01
Medical Assisting

This course provides a focus on clinical procedures and practices. Students will learn about the responsibilities and duties of medical assistants, anatomy and physiology of the human body, and the importance of maintaining a clean and safe clinical environment. Students will become familiar with vital signs measurement, phlebotomy, sterilization and infection control, and basic laboratory procedures.

02
Fundamentals of Medical Specialties

This course provides students with an overview of the various medical specialties and subspecialties within the field of medicine. Through lectures, discussions, and case studies, students will gain a deeper understanding of specialized areas including cardiology, oncology, neurology, and more â as well as the role of medical assistants in each specialty.
