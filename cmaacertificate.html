Professional Milestone

The CMAA Credential

Your institutional certificate from AlliedRx signifies that you have mastered the administrative workflow of a clinical environment.

Why it matters:

  • Verification: Employers can verify your training through our database.
  • Career Entry: Essential for applying to medical coordinator and front-office roles.
  • Exam Prerequisite: Proves eligibility for the national NHA certification exam.
Certificate Storage Tip:
  • Keep your digital PDF on a secure cloud drive.
  • Add the AlliedRx training to your LinkedIn "Education" section.
  • Provide a copy to your NHA testing site if requested.
