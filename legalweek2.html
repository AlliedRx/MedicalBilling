🔒 Scenario 2: Patient Privacy Breach
While at lunch, you overhear a coworker discussing a patient's HIV diagnosis with another employee in the hospital cafeteria. What should you do?
📋 Scenario 3: Scope of Practice Boundary
A patient asks you if a particular prescription medication is appropriate for their condition. No physician is currently available in the clinic. The patient seems to be waiting for your medical opinion. What is the legally appropriate response?
