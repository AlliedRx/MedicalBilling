Our Medical Billing & Coding Specialist program prepares you for one of the most in-demand entry-level healthcare careers. Medical Billing & Coding technicians collect blood samples from patients for lab analysis, working in hospitals, clinics, blood banks, and doctors' offices. You will learn venipuncture techniques, infection control, patient communication, and laboratory procedures.

After completing the program, you will be fully prepared to sit for the NHA Certified Medical Biller & Coder (CPT) exam — a nationally recognized credential that opens doors to immediate employment in healthcare settings across the country.

Curriculum

2 Courses · Complete Curriculum

Each course builds on the last — from foundational phlebotomy knowledge to advanced venipuncture techniques and specimen processing.

01
Medical Billing & Coding

This course provides an introduction to the fundamental principles of phlebotomy, the practice of collecting blood specimens from patients for laboratory analysis. Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the phlebotomy process, from preparation and patient interaction to blood collection techniques and safety measures. Covers anatomy and physiology of the circulatory system, types of blood specimens, ethical and legal considerations, and maintaining a sterile and safe environment during blood collection.

02
Medical Billing & Coding Lab

This course provides hands-on training in the practical application of phlebotomy techniques. Students will develop the skills necessary to perform safe and effective blood draws, including proper patient preparation, vein selection, and use of various blood collection equipment. Emphasis is placed on real-world clinical scenarios and exam preparation for the NCCT CBCS certification exam.
