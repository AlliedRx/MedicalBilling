About Army CA
The Army Credentialing Assistance (CA) program provides funding for active duty Army Soldiers to earn civilian credentials, licenses, and certifications that relate to their Military Occupational Specialty (MOS). Soldiers can receive up to $4,000 per fiscal year to pay for approved training programs and certification exams. AlliedRx's healthcare programs are approved under Army CA, allowing active duty Soldiers to earn their healthcare credentials at no out-of-pocket cost. The program covers both the training program and the national certification exam fees through a streamlined reimbursement process via ArmyIgnitED.
Benefit Details
  • Up to $4,000 per fiscal year for credentialing
  • Covers both training program fees and exam costs
  • Available to active duty Army Soldiers
  • MOS-aligned programs receive priority funding
  • Administered through ArmyIgnitED portal
  • No minimum service requirement for initial enrollment

About AF COOL
The Air Force Credentialing Opportunities On-Line (AF COOL) program helps active duty Air Force and Space Force personnel earn civilian credentials and certifications that map to their Air Force Specialty Codes (AFSCs). The program funds the cost of approved credential exams and, in many cases, associated preparatory training. AlliedRx programs are recognized under AF COOL, giving Airmen and Guardians a clear pathway to earning civilian healthcare credentials while on active duty. This is especially valuable for those in medical, administrative, and technical AFSCs who plan to transition to civilian healthcare careers.

  • Covers training prep programs and exam registration
  • Available to active duty Air Force & Space Force members
  • AFSC crosswalk tool helps identify eligible credentials
  • No out-of-pocket cost for approved programs
  • Supports transition to civilian healthcare employment
